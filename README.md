# \# Business Acquisition





# \## Business Problem Overview and KPIs

# 

# Two companies are joining forces. For a smooth transition, the following parameters need to be checked to audit the business structure of the company:

# 

# \- \*\*How much did company sell and how many products did it sell?

# \- \*\*How much money did it make and what was the profit margin?

# \- \*\*We need last year's detailed performance for comparison.

# \- \*\*Compare this year's performance with last year's.

# \- \*\*Look at the average profit and sales trends.

# \- \*\*Break down the analysis by quarters.

# \- \*\*Compare cumulative sales this year versus last year.

# \- \*\*Identify the top-performing products and top customers.

# \- \*\*Analyze sales performance by region, sales channel, city, and product names.

# \- \*\*Track the average profit and sales trends over time.
